## 1. Introduction

Medena Care connects you with board-certified dermatologists for online skin consultations. Whether you have a rash, mole concern, or need a prescription renewal, you can get professional medical advice from the comfort of your home.

### Key Benefits
- **Fast response**: Receive a diagnosis within 24-48 hours
- **Convenient**: No waiting rooms or travel required
- **Professional**: All consultations reviewed by licensed dermatologists
- **Secure**: Your data is protected with medical-grade security

## 2. Creating Your Account

### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Visit the website** at [medena.care](https://medena.care)
2. **Click "Get Started"** or "Start Consultation" on the homepage
3. **Select "Sign Up"** tab on the authentication page
4. **Enter your details**:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm password
5. **Click "Sign Up"** to create your account
6. **Check your email** for a verification link
7. **Click the verification link** to activate your account
8. **Log in** with your email and password

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- We recommend using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

### Forgot Your Password?
1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password

## 3. Starting a Skin Consultation

The consultation process guides you through 10 simple steps to provide your dermatologist with all the information they need.

### Step 1: Select Your Concern

Choose the category that best matches your skin issue:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Skin Conditions** | Rashes, eczema, psoriasis, acne, and other skin issues |
| **Hair & Scalp** | Hair loss, dandruff, scalp conditions |
| **Nail Problems** | Fungal infections, nail changes, discoloration |
| **Infections** | Suspected bacterial, viral, or fungal infections |
| **Allergies & Reactions** | Allergic reactions, hives, contact dermatitis |
| **Pigmentation** | Moles, dark spots, skin discoloration |

### Step 2: Mark the Affected Area

Tap on the body diagram to indicate where your skin issue is located. You can select multiple areas if needed.

### Step 3: Photo Guidelines

Review the guidelines for taking clear photos of your skin concern:
- Use good lighting (natural daylight is best)
- Keep the camera steady and in focus
- Include a close-up and a wider view
- Avoid using flash directly on the skin

### Step 4: Upload Photos

Upload 2-4 photos of your skin concern:
1. Click "Upload Photos" or drag and drop your images
2. Ensure photos are clear and well-lit
3. Include both close-up and overview shots
4. Add labels if helpful (e.g., "main area", "spreading")

**Tip**: Better photos lead to more accurate diagnoses.

### Step 5: Timeline & Changes

Answer questions about your condition:
- When did you first notice the issue?
- Has it changed or spread over time?
- If yes, describe how it has changed

### Step 6: Symptoms

Select any symptoms you're experiencing:
- Itching
- Pain or tenderness
- Burning sensation
- Swelling
- Bleeding
- Discharge

Rate your symptom severity on a scale.

### Step 7: Medical History

Provide relevant medical information:
- **Allergies**: List any known allergies (medications, substances)
- **Medications**: Current medications you're taking
- **Self-treatment**: Any creams, ointments, or remedies you've tried

### Step 8: Choose Your Plan

Select your consultation urgency:

| Plan | Response Time | Price | Best For |
|------|--------------|-------|----------|
| **Standard** | Within 48 hours (weekdays) | €49 | Non-urgent concerns |
| **Urgent** | Within 24 hours (including weekends) | €74 | Urgent assessments |

Both plans include:
- Professional dermatologist assessment
- Prescription if medically necessary
- Home delivery of medication (if prescribed)

The Urgent plan also includes one follow-up question.

### Step 9: Account & Payment

1. **Create account or log in** if you haven't already
2. **Enter patient details**:
   - Date of birth
   - Biological sex (for medical accuracy)
   - Address (for prescription delivery)
3. **Proceed to payment** via secure Stripe checkout
4. **Complete payment** using credit/debit card

### Step 10: Confirmation

You'll see a confirmation screen with:
- Your consultation reference number
- Expected response time
- Next steps

You'll also receive a confirmation email with these details.

## 4. Requesting a Prescription

If you need to renew an existing prescription without a full consultation:

1. **Start a new consultation**
2. **Select "Request Prescription"** (shown below the skin concern categories)
3. **Choose your pricing plan** (€29 for prescription requests)
4. **Complete account details and payment**
5. **Submit your request**

**Important**: Prescription requests are for renewals only. Your doctor will review your request and issue the prescription if appropriate.

### What's Included
- Prescription issued within 24 hours
- Uploaded to your e-Medikation (ELGA)
- No consultation or new diagnosis included

## 6. Viewing Your Results

### Accessing Your Dashboard

1. **Log in** at [medena.care/auth/patient](https://medena.care/auth/patient)
2. **View your dashboard** showing all consultations

### Understanding Consultation Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Submitted** | Your consultation is awaiting review |
| **In Review** | A doctor is currently reviewing your case |
| **Completed** | Your consultation has been answered |
| **Cancelled** | The consultation was cancelled |

### Reading Your Doctor's Response

When your consultation is completed:
1. Click on the consultation in your dashboard
2. View the doctor's assessment, including:
   - Diagnosis (with ICD-10 code)
   - Recommended treatment
   - Prescription details (if issued)
   - Follow-up advice

## 7. Downloading Reports

### Consultation Report (Befundbericht)

After your consultation is completed:
1. Open the consultation from your dashboard
2. Click **"Download Consultation Report"**
3. The PDF will download containing:
   - Summary of your submitted information
   - Doctor's assessment and diagnosis
   - Treatment recommendations

The report is available in your current app language (English or German).

### Fee Note (Honorarnote)

For insurance reimbursement:
1. Open a completed consultation
2. Click **"Download Fee Note"** (Honorarnote herunterladen)
3. Submit to your insurance provider

## 9. Using a Doctor's Referral Link

If your doctor has given you a referral link:

### How It Works

1. **Click the referral link** provided by your doctor
2. **You'll see a banner** showing you're referred by that doctor
3. **Complete your consultation** as normal
4. **Your case goes directly to that doctor** for review

### Benefits of Referral Links
- Cases are assigned directly to your chosen doctor
- May have customized pricing
- Personalized welcome message from your doctor
- Continuity of care with a doctor who knows your history

### Referral Link Format
Referral links look like: `medena.care/consultation?ref=DRSMITH`

## 10. FAQs

### Account & Access

**Q: I didn't receive a verification email. What should I do?**
A: Check your spam folder. If not there, try signing up again or contact support.

**Q: Can I use Medena Care on my phone?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully mobile-responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use medical-grade security and comply with Austrian data protection regulations.

### Consultations

**Q: How long until I receive a response?**
A: Standard consultations: within 48 hours (weekdays). Urgent: within 24 hours (including weekends).

**Q: What if I need to add more information after submitting?**
A: Contact your assigned doctor through the follow-up feature (available with Urgent plan) or start a new consultation.

**Q: Can I get a prescription?**
A: Yes, if the doctor determines it's medically necessary. Prescriptions are uploaded to e-Medikation (ELGA).

**Q: What conditions can be treated?**
A: Most skin conditions can be assessed. For emergencies or conditions requiring physical examination, your doctor will refer you to in-person care.

### Payments & Insurance

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds may be available in certain circumstances. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: Will my insurance cover this?**
A: Many Austrian insurers reimburse telemedicine. Download your Honorarnote and submit it to your provider.

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe with bank-level encryption.
